What is Skin Cancer?

Skin cancer is the abnormal growth of skin cells, most often developing on skin exposed to the sun. The three main types are:

Melanoma

The most serious type, developing in melanocytes (pigment-producing cells)

Basal Cell Carcinoma

The most common type, appearing in basal cells of the skin

Squamous Cell Carcinoma

Develops in squamous cells in the outer layer of skin
